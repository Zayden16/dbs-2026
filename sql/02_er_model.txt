Conceptual ER Model
====================

Entity Sets, Relationship Sets, and Attributes for the "Are EVs Really Better?" project.
This model integrates data from 4 independent source groups into a normalized schema (3NF).

================================================================================
ENTITY SETS
================================================================================

1. COUNTRY
   - country_code (PK, CHAR(2))       — ISO 2-letter code: CH, DE, FR
   - country_name (VARCHAR(100))       — Switzerland, Germany, France
   - petrol_price_eur_per_l (DECIMAL)  — current petrol price
   - diesel_price_eur_per_l (DECIMAL)  — current diesel price
   Purpose: Central reference entity linking all country-level data

2. VEHICLE
   - vehicle_id (PK, INT AUTO_INCREMENT)
   - make (VARCHAR(50))                — manufacturer (BMW, Tesla, etc.)
   - model (VARCHAR(100))              — model name
   - fuel_type (VARCHAR(30))           — Electric, Petrol, Diesel, Hybrid, etc.
   - vehicle_class (VARCHAR(50))       — SUV, Compact, Sedan, etc.
   - price_usd (DECIMAL)              — approximate price in USD
   - horsepower (INT)                  — engine/motor power in hp
   - seats (INT)                       — number of seats
   Purpose: Unified vehicle catalog for both ICE and EV

3. ICE_EMISSION
   - ice_emission_id (PK, INT AUTO_INCREMENT)
   - vehicle_id (FK -> VEHICLE)
   - engine_size_l (DECIMAL)           — engine displacement in liters
   - cylinders (INT)                   — number of cylinders
   - transmission (VARCHAR(10))        — transmission type code
   - fuel_consumption_city (DECIMAL)   — L/100km city
   - fuel_consumption_hwy (DECIMAL)    — L/100km highway
   - fuel_consumption_comb (DECIMAL)   — L/100km combined
   - co2_emissions_gkm (INT)          — CO2 in g/km (tailpipe)
   Purpose: ICE-specific emissions and fuel consumption data

4. EV_SPEC
   - ev_spec_id (PK, INT AUTO_INCREMENT)
   - vehicle_id (FK -> VEHICLE)
   - battery_capacity_kwh (DECIMAL)    — battery size in kWh
   - range_wltp_km (INT)              — WLTP range in km
   - energy_consumption_kwh_100km (DECIMAL) — derived: kWh/100km
   - dc_charge_power_kw (INT)         — DC fast charging power
   - ac_charge_power_kw (DECIMAL)     — AC charging power
   - release_year (INT)               — year of market release
   Purpose: EV-specific specs and energy consumption

5. CHARGING_STATION
   - station_id (PK, INT)             — from source data
   - name (VARCHAR(255))              — station name
   - city (VARCHAR(100))              — city name
   - state_province (VARCHAR(100))    — state/province/canton
   - country_code (FK -> COUNTRY)
   - latitude (DECIMAL(10,6))         — GPS lat
   - longitude (DECIMAL(10,6))        — GPS lon
   - ports (INT)                      — number of ports
   - power_kw (DECIMAL)              — charging power
   - power_class (VARCHAR(30))        — AC_L1, AC_L2, DC_FAST, DC_ULTRA
   - is_fast_dc (BOOLEAN)            — has DC fast charging
   Purpose: Individual charging station locations and capabilities

6. CHARGING_COUNTRY_SUMMARY
   - country_code (PK, FK -> COUNTRY)
   - station_count (INT)              — total stations
   - port_count (INT)                 — total ports
   - fast_station_share (DECIMAL)     — fraction of fast-charging stations
   - fast_port_share (DECIMAL)        — fraction of fast-charging ports
   Purpose: Aggregated charging infrastructure per country

7. ELECTRICITY_PRICE
   - elec_price_id (PK, INT AUTO_INCREMENT)
   - country_code (FK -> COUNTRY)
   - year (INT)                        — year
   - half_year (TINYINT)              — 1 or 2 (S1/S2), NULL for annual
   - price_eur_per_kwh (DECIMAL)      — price in EUR/kWh (converted for CH)
   Purpose: Household electricity prices over time, unified across sources

8. GRID_MIX
   - grid_mix_id (PK, INT AUTO_INCREMENT)
   - country_code (FK -> COUNTRY)
   - year (INT)
   - source_type (VARCHAR(30))         — Coal, Gas, Nuclear, Hydro, Wind, Solar, etc.
   - generation_twh (DECIMAL)          — electricity generated in TWh
   - share_pct (DECIMAL)              — % share of total generation
   - co2_intensity_gkwh (DECIMAL)     — gCO2/kWh for this source (if available)
   Purpose: Electricity generation mix per country per year

9. EV_ADOPTION
   - ev_adoption_id (PK, INT AUTO_INCREMENT)
   - country_code (FK -> COUNTRY)
   - year (INT)
   - powertrain (VARCHAR(10))          — BEV, PHEV, or EV (total)
   - ev_sales (INT)                    — number of EVs sold
   - ev_stock (INT)                    — total EVs on the road
   - ev_sales_share_pct (DECIMAL)     — % of total car sales
   - ev_stock_share_pct (DECIMAL)     — % of total car stock
   Purpose: EV market penetration over time per country

================================================================================
RELATIONSHIP SETS
================================================================================

COUNTRY ---< CHARGING_STATION        (1:N) — a country has many stations
COUNTRY ---  CHARGING_COUNTRY_SUMMARY (1:1) — one summary per country
COUNTRY ---< ELECTRICITY_PRICE       (1:N) — prices over multiple periods
COUNTRY ---< GRID_MIX                (1:N) — mix entries per year per source
COUNTRY ---< EV_ADOPTION             (1:N) — adoption data per year

VEHICLE ---  ICE_EMISSION             (1:1) — ICE vehicles have emission data
VEHICLE ---  EV_SPEC                  (1:1) — EVs have spec data

Note: VEHICLE is the integration point between the car datasets.
      COUNTRY is the integration point between all country-level datasets.
